Arcade Challenge

Cross The Bridge

Trust your guts, and make it across without stepping on the wrong glass.

How to Play

  1. Each row has two glass tiles, but only one is safe.
  2. Tap one tile in the highlighted front row to jump forward.
  3. If you pick the wrong tile, it breaks and the run ends instantly.
  4. You get 2 pebbles. Tap PEB, then tap a front tile to test it from a distance.
  5. A safe tile is marked for you. A wrong test costs a pebble.
  6. Reach the finish platform to win. Use reset to reroll the bridge, or home to come back here.
